My name is Ryan Bolson, I'm a digital artist, animator, and illustrator.

Art has been my life for the past five years and during that time I

ventured to master my fundamentals, gain experience, and develop both

my hand and eye artistically. I tackle a variety of mediums including

digital painting, digital line-art and pixel art, as well as a mixture

of styles and subject matters, mostly leaning towards a modern-eastern

inspired style of drawing, with influences from a handful of well know

digital artists.
